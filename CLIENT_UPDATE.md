# Skill Trade, Progress Update

Prepared for James Atkinson, June 2026

## Where we are

The heart of the platform is built and working. A customer can post a job,
the right local tradespeople get matched to it, and a trade can unlock the
lead and contact the customer directly. You can click through the whole
journey on the live site today.

In plan terms, that covers Phase 1 (Foundation) and Phase 2 (the Core loop).

## For your customers

- Post a job in a couple of minutes, with no account needed. A simple
  step-by-step form with a review screen before it goes live.
- Choose how they want to be contacted: WhatsApp, phone, or email.
- Get a private link to track the job, see which trades have picked it up,
  and mark it done or cancel it.
- A small map shows the job's area.

## For tradespeople

- A personalised job feed showing local work that matches their trade and area.
- They choose how far they will travel (your question, answered below).
- Each job shows how urgent it is and how many trades have already claimed it.
- They unlock a job to reveal the customer's details, then get in touch with
  one tap.
- A dashboard tracks their unlocks and the jobs they have taken on.

## The promise that keeps it fair

- A job is only ever shared with a maximum of five trades. That cap is locked
  in at the core of the system, so a lead can never be oversold.
- Higher subscription tiers see new jobs sooner. That early access is the main
  reason a trade pays, and it means we never take commission on their work.

## Your question about distance

You asked whether trades could choose their own range rather than being fixed
to one area. Done. Each trade now picks how far they will travel, from 5km up
to 100km, or "Anywhere in Ireland" for companies that cover the whole country.
Nobody is boxed in.

## Trust and quality (in progress)

A few of the trust features are partly in place and get completed in the later
phases:

- The verified badge and subscription tier already show wherever a trade
  appears.
- AI checking of job posts, so spam or nonsense never reaches trades, is
  designed and ready to build. It would cost only a few euro a month to run.
- A report system for bad or dead leads is on the list.

## What is next

- Phase 3, Billing and accounts: switching on payments so trades can subscribe,
  plus the verification process. This is the step that turns it into a live,
  earning business.
- Phase 4, Reviews and ranking: customer reviews, ratings on profiles, and a
  local leaderboard.
- Phase 5, Launch and hardening: full testing and a supported go-live.

## In short

The engine of Skill Trade is running. The next phase switches on payments, and
from there we add the reviews and reputation layer. Happy to walk you through
the live site whenever suits.
